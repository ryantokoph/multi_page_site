Which type of Kayak is right for you?



Sit-In Style

The sit-in style of Kayak is a favorite of many. It offers much more storage capacity than a sit-on style Kayak.
It also shields your legs from the sun during the summer, offering cooler temperatures for the rider.
Many enthusiasts who kayak in rapid waters use some variation of sit-in kayak.




Sit-On-Top Style

The sit-on style of kayak is a great choice for anyone who likes to soak up the sun.
It is also a great choice for beginners because while it's easier to flip over than a sit-in,
it's actually much easier to get back into because the boat doesn't fill with water.
These are also great for fishng!
