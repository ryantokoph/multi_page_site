What to Bring on a Kayak Trip



These a few of the essentials to bring:


  • A dry bag

    • - A dry bag is important for storing valuables in case you flip


  • A waterproof phone case

    • - Don't want to accidentally splash your phone


  • A life vest

    • - It adds a layer of cushion to your back (plus it's required by law!)


  • A bluetooth speaker

    • - Nothing is better than listening to your favorite music while on the water
