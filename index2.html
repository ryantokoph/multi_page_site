Best Kayaking Spots in NC


Here's some of my favorite spots to 'yak around NC



Lake Wylie

Lake Wylie stretches into both North and South Carolina. It's a man-made lake with lots
of motorboat traffic, but if you paddle into the coves, there's still an abundace of wildlife to see.
If you're into fishingm there are plenty of spots to cast a line in on the shore or even right on the water!



Catawba River

The Catawba moves a bit faster than Lake Wylie, but there's no need to worry about any harsh rapids.
There are a few bumpy spots with some rocks to watch out for, but for the most part, it's smooth
paddling. Not to mention a number of great restaurants right along the riverbank!
